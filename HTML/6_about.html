About Access LAYA

Our Mission

Access LAYA strives to serve as a fundamental and accessible digital platform built on static HTML and CSS, dedicated to combating Gender-Based Violence (GBV). We strive to empower visitors by transforming awareness into actionable steps through our core framework: Learn, Act, Yoke, Advocate (LAYA). Our purpose is to provide clear, reliable, and consolidated educational resources on the forms, causes, and prevention of violence affecting individuals of all genders. We are committed to equipping every user with the knowledge and tools necessary to champion equality and contribute to a world free from violence.

Our Story

Access LAYA was born from the shared commitment of three female Computer Science students who recognized that technology could be a powerful tool in the fight for social justice. Faced with the persistent reality of violence against all genders, we channeled our technical skills and passion for advocacy into creating this website.

Access LAYA is more than just code; it is an evolving movement. It is where you Learn about the issues, find the courage to Act, stand in solidarity and Yoke with the community, and find the resources to Advocate for systemic change. Our story is the story of students using their skills to make a difference. We invite you to make this platform part of your own journey in creating a safer, more equitable world.

Our Four Pillars

Access LAYA’s approach is built on four interconnected pillars that guide our work and create meaningful, sustainable change.

L

Learn

The Learn Page transitions the user to essential educational content. This pillar is dedicated to explaining the diverse forms of gender-based violence and detailing their underlying social and political causes.

A

Act

The Act Page focuses on practical and immediate engagement. It demonstrates immediate action steps, practical prevention advice, actionable strategies, and direct hotlines for urgent assistance.

Y

Yoke

The Yoke Page is centered on fostering solidarity and community support. This section loads real-life content, showcasing survivor testimonials, advocate profiles, and available community support resources.

A

Advocate

The Advocate Page empowers users to drive systemic change. It displays current campaigns and offers action items such as ways to get involved, sign petitions, share information, and download advocacy materials.
